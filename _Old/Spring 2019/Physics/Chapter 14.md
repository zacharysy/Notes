## Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM)
when
$$a\propto-x$$
$$F\propto(x-x_0)$$

### Mass on a Spring
$$F = -kx$$
$$a=-\frac{k}{m}x$$

### Functions
where $A$ is the amplitude, and $\varphi$ is the phase.
#### Displacement
$$x(t)=A\cos(\omega t + \varphi)$$

#### Velocity
$$v(t)=-A\omega\sin(\omega t)$$

$$v_{max} = \omega A$$

#### Acceleration
$$a(t)=-A\omega^2\cos(\omega t)$$

$$A_{max} = -\omega^2A$$

### Variables
#### Angular Frequency 
$$\omega = 2\pi f = \frac{2\pi}{T}$$
$$f=\frac{1}{T}$$

#### For mass on spring
$$\omega = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}$$
$$f=\frac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}$$

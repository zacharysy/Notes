# 25: Current, Resistance, and Electromotive Force

## Current
* $$I=\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta t}$$
* constant in a series

## Resistance
* $$R=\frac{\rho L}{A}$$

## EMF
* A potential difference that's set up in a circuit, like in a battery
* $$V_{ab}=\mathcal{E}-Ir$$
* $$I=\frac{\mathcal{E}}{R+r}$$
